LIST OF SOME HTML TAGS

My favorite ones in order:

Anchor tag (<a></a>)
It is the most fun tag we have learned so far. It allows you to link stuff to your page from the web.
LIKE THIS!
Image tag (<img>)
This is the one I used, to show my cat's photo on my page. Shouldn't forget to use it with src and alt attributes though.
Pharagraph Tag (<p></p>)
I like it cuz it's simple and probably carries most of the content inside.
Article Tag (<article></article>)
Seperates different pieces of content in your website.
Heading Size Tags (<h1></h1>)
Determines the heading size and importance based on its number
